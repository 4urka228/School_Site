About us

Hrebinky Lyceum is a comprehensive educational institution located in the urban-type settlement of Hrebinky, serving students from the local community and surrounding areas. Situated at 23 Nauky Avenue, our school has been a cornerstone of education since its establishment, providing quality learning opportunities for children and teens

Our Mission

As an educational and training complex, we offer a complete educational pathway, nurturing students through their formative years with dedicated teachers and modern learning approaches. We combine traditional educational values with contemporary teaching methods.

Our Approach

We create an environment where students can develop academically, socially, and personally. Our lyceum is committed to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning in every student who walks through our doors.

Our Community

We believe in strong partnerships between students, teachers, and families. Our school community works together to create meaningful learning experiences and celebrate achievements that extend far beyond the classroom.
